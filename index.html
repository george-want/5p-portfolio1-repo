About Rainy City Tours

Hello, and thanks for checking us out! Raincy City Tours is a company dedicated to giving you the most authentic experiences that Manchester has to offer.

We offer tours led by proper Mancunians designed to get you under the skin of the city. We know the tourist version isn't what you want - you want to see the guts and the soul of the original 'dirty old town'.

So check out our tours and see which one suits you best. Whether it's one of our three public tours or you want something a bit more personal, we have an experience for you. Get in touch, let's show you around!

Manchester City Centre

Looking for the perfect intro to Manchester? Look no further. We'll show you around the city's industrial past and metropolitan present.

Manchester Music Legends

You wanna roll with it? You wanna take your time? Join our evening tour for the best in Manchester's historied music scene.

Manchester Pub Crawl

Sure, you can look around, but there's only one way to really get to know Manchester - drinking pints in the city's best historic pubs.
